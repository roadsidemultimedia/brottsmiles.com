Nathan Brott, DDS

Dr. Brott graduated from the University of the Pacific Dental School in 1995. After one year of advanced residency training he moved to Grass Valley and began private practice. Dr. Brott has completed several advanced education courses and is a graduate of the prestigious Pacific Aesthetic Continuum, which focuses on state-of-the-art aesthetic and restorative techniques. He is dedicated to continuing his studies with the leaders in dentistry so he can bring the best options available to his patients. Dr. Brott is passionate about getting to know his patients and helping them discover the many gifts that modern dentistry has to offer. His interests include mountain biking, fly fishing, and spending time with his family.
